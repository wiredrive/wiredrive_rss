RSS Examples

All Wiredrive presentations give you the option of create a corresponding mRSS feed, which contains the media, thumbnail images and metadata in your presentation. Web developers can then use this feed to populate your website with content from any of your Wiredrive presentations.

The example projects below illustrate how you can retrieve and display the information from your Wiredrive presentations.

For more information, see the Guide to Wiredrive media RSS page at the Wiredrive Support Center
